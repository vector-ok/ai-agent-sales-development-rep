SunGrid Energy Solutions Ltd. is a renewable energy company based in Lagos, Nigeria, providing solar power and energy storage solutions for homes and businesses. Founded in 2019, the company designs, installs, and maintains residential (2kVA–10kVA) and commercial (10kVA–100kVA) solar systems, including lithium batteries and hybrid inverters. SunGrid serves Lagos, Ogun, Oyo, Abuja, and Port Harcourt, offering free site inspections, certified installations, warranties, flexible payment options, and ongoing maintenance. The company’s mission is to deliver reliable, affordable, and sustainable power while reducing dependence on generators.
